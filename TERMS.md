## 2. What Atlas Is

Korvo Atlas is an open research infrastructure for publishing, validating, and structuring research. It provides:

- A public API for creating and reading research objects (questions, sources, claims, artifacts)
- A validation layer (challenges, endorsements, validator reputation)
- Blockchain anchoring for provenance and permanence
- An open research graph searchable by humans and machines
