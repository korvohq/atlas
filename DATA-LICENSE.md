## Research Data License

All public research data published through the Korvo Atlas canonical hub — including questions, sources, claims, artifacts, endorsements, and challenges — is made available under:

### Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-SA 4.0)

https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/

**You are free to:**

- **Share** — copy and redistribute the data in any medium or format
- **Adapt** — remix, transform, and build upon the data for any purpose, including commercial

**Under the following terms:**

- **Attribution** — You must give appropriate credit to "Korvo Atlas" and provide a link to the original data. You must indicate if changes were made.
- **ShareAlike** — If you remix, transform, or build upon the data, you must distribute your contributions under the same or a compatible license.

## Database License

The compiled Korvo Atlas research graph — the structured collection of all objects and their relationships as a whole — is additionally protected under the:

### Open Database License (ODbL) v1.0

https://opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/1-0/

**You are free to:**

- **Share** — copy, distribute, and use the database
- **Create** — produce works from the database
- **Adapt** — modify, transform, and build upon the database

**Provided that you:**

- **Attribute** — credit "Korvo Atlas" in any public use of the database
- **Share-Alike** — if you publicly use an adapted version of the database, offer it under ODbL as well
- **Keep open** — if you redistribute the database, do not use technical measures (e.g. DRM) that restrict others from exercising their ODbL rights

## AI Training & Bulk Data Restrictions

Use of Korvo Atlas data for training machine learning models, large language models, or similar AI systems requires a separate **Data Access License**.

Automated bulk scraping, crawling, or systematic downloading of the Korvo Atlas API or database for the purpose of building competing products or training AI models is **not permitted** under the CC BY-SA 4.0 or ODbL licenses without explicit written permission from Snab Limited (trading as Korvo).

To inquire about a Data Access License, contact: **data@korvo.io**

## Self-Hosted Instances

Operators running their own Atlas instances are free to choose their own data licensing terms for research published on their node. The terms above apply to the canonical public hub operated by Snab Limited (trading as Korvo) at `atlas.korvo.io`.
